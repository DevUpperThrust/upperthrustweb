What We Are

MEA - A WORLD OF OPPORTUNITIES

Maratha Entrepreneurs Association (MEA) is an initiative to bring individuals belonging to the Maratha entrepreneur community under a single umbrella, thereby facilitating their business growth.

MEA was set up with the wider mission of empowering community entrepreneur and adding value to their business ventures.

It is a potential platform where entrepreneur will benefit in multiple ways -

Business knowledge sharing

Enhance the knowledge about their business domain(s)

Strength of unity

Leverage the collective power of a group

Trust building

Build long-lasting & symbiotic relationships

Business promotion

Promote business through a prospective audience

Enriched social structure

Contribute towards strengthening the social fabric

Mentoring

GUIDANCE FROM DISTINGUISHED GUEST

Learning is a continuous process that broadens our horizon, equips us with new skills and Strengthens our capabilities. Even a small, passionate speech by accomplished entrepreneur will go a long way in motivating members.

MEA would invite highly successful individuals who have made immense contribution in their chosen field of business. Words of wisdom and guidance from such eminent personalities will serve as a source of inspiration for our members, especially budding entrepreneurs.

Workshop will usually be schedule every 90 days and will be open to members only.
